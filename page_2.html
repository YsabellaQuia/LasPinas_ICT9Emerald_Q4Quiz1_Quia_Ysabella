Characters In Noli Me Tangere

Characters In Noli Me Tangere

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • A student who recently returned to the Philippines from Europe after spending seven years studying abroad
  • An earnest and idealistic young man. Influenced by his European education, he's trying to improve the country
Maria Clara de los Santos
  • The daughter of Capitan Santiago and the fiancee of Ibarra.
  • A kindhearted woman, that is well-loved by everyone around her.
Elias
  • A fugitive living in San Diego after renouncing his wealth.
  • Is a selfless man man bound by a code of honor.
Kapitan Tiago
  • An influential business man and the father of Maria Clara.
  • Is described to be a weak and submissive man.
Padre Damaso
  • The former town curate and the real father of Maria Clara
  • Is a brash, arrogant and rude individual. He acts superior to others.
Padre Salvi
  • Was a town curate and is the successor of Padre Damaso. Is also at odds with the town's alfarez.
  • Is a cunning man who uses his religous status for political influence.
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He's the father of Juan Crisostomo Ibarra. He went agaisnt the elite.
  • Is a virtuous and generous man.
Pilosopo Tasyo o Don Anastacio
  • An old scholar and is often thought of as crazy.
  • He's an intelligent individual. Even debating religous concepts with others.
Sisa
  • The mother of Basilio and Crispin.
  • A devoted mother to her children but is too submissive to stand up to her husband.
Crispin
  • A sacristan of the church and the brother of Basilio.
  • A clever young boy.
Basilio
  • A sacristan of the church and the older brother of Crispin.
  • An optimistic young boy, despite living in a hard situation.
Donya Victorina de Espadaña
  • A socialite and friend of Kapitan Tiago. Is the wife of Tibrucio.
  • Is a woman lies about her identity to gain respect.
Don Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • Is the husband of Donya Victorina
  • He is submissive towards his wife.
Donya Consolacion
  • An older woman married to Alperes.
  • A brutal wife to her husband, who berates him and encourages his rivarly. Also prefers being referred to
Alperes
  • He's in charge of the Civil Guard in San Diego.
  • A cruel man who tortures his soldiers and flaunts his power.
Tiya Isabel
  • The cousin of Kapitan Tiago.
  • The woman who raised Maria Clara after her mother died.
